Frequently Asked Questions

What if I miss a class?

+

Our class timings are 8 PM to 10:30 PM. In case you miss a class, you can watch the recording later. If you miss multiple classes, you can switch to another Batch*.

What if I am not able to manage my job or schedule with the class timings?

+

You can complete the course or program at your own pace through lecture recordings and learning materials. We focus on each student individually, ensuring our instructors match the pace of the batch.

Do I get lifetime access?

+

We offer 12 months of access to the recordings. After that period, access will be revoked, but you can extend it for an additional 12 months by paying ₹599
